History of Angoshova

Angoshova was established in 1991 but actually it has started its journey from 1979 as different name. This business was run in 2 ways whole sale and retail. The business policy of Angoshova was running generation to generation. It’s founder Late Abdul Latif Bhuyan father of Mr. Md. Selim Bhuyan & Mr. Md. Firoz Alam Bhuyan. The cloth Business was ruined almost 40 years by Late Abdul Latif Bhuyan. The business policy of Angoshova was running according to his direction till before 1991. In 1991 Angoshova giving up the whole sale business started to in retail readymade garments item. When Angoshova started like this business, it had only a 270sft shop taken by 3 Employee. Readymade products like Shirt, Pant, Panjabi, Sweater, Shari, Three-piece and other goods used to collect from Dhaka and other Jones.

All time Angoshova gives the importance in the modern design, standard good and qualities cloths. At present by the grace of Almighty Allah “Angoshova” is running by taking 35 Employees in the three floors building which is 8,500sft shop.

The present Angoshova which we are looking it’s started in January, 2014 in new pattern and design. In the inauguration ceremony of the new building was present Mr. Badiuzzaman sir, Ex-Chairman of Anti-Corruption Commission of Bangladesh, The Deputy Commissioner (DC) and other Administrative officer also were present there.

Angoshova imports different kind of products from different Countries and also the domestic products. Cosmetics, Ornaments, Lathered Bag, Shoes, Bata exclusive showroom and various kinds of foreign & domestic products added with the readymade items. In the last years the name & fame popularity and goodwill of Angoshova speeded not only in the district of Lakshmipur but also in the remote arias of Bangladesh.

For this the demand of its products redoubled & lighted countrywide. So Angoshova started Online based E-commerce service. After starting the online delivery service Angoshova is supplying the different kinds of products through the courier service almost all districts of the country.

Today Angoshova is self-sufficient Business within thirty years. Its continuity shall be run in the future In-sha-Allah. The future plan of Angoshova is to arrange the employment for the large workless number of people and will establish Industries, Educational institutions, Hospital etc. and will give the free education & free service to enrich Bangladesh.
