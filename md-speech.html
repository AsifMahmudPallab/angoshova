Managing Director Speech

I would like to take this opportunity introduce myself as a Managing Director of “Angoshova Limited”. Very beginning we started as a Retails Business Since 1991; in response to now we are our business area including ready-made garments item, shoes, cosmetics, Construction business, export and import as well as limited company.

On this auspicious day, I recall the contributions of all members of the “Angoshova” family who are with us and also to those who are not with “Angoshova Limited”, but their contributions are remember with gratitude. The Company continues to develop a well balanced business portfolio which is not only depended on any particular type of business or client. The Company is working to introduce new products and services. At the start of our company was facing an uncomfortable financial position, business uncertainty in many of our markets and severe challenges threatening our future economic and financial performance. The main priority was to oppose and reverse the trend towards a worsening financial position of the company. As Managing Director, my role was to describe those challenges and difficulties. I also wanted to convey, with that same clarity and conviction, that we were entirely confident and certain about the path we had to follow for the company successfully to overcome those many challenges. We want to change peoples’ lives for the better by providing them opportunities and by creating an environment that drives efficiency and productivity.

I would like to give thank to the management team and all professionals for the extraordinary efforts and performance they have put in day after day to transform and improve our company and make it more competitive for their continuous support and realizing our vision and goal. May Allah shower his mercy on us all and give us the strength and capacity to make our future dream comes true.

Tank’s to all

Md. Firoz Alam

Managing Director
Angoshova Ltd.
